## Part B: TikTok launch (new account)

TikTok does not exist for Kate yet. The Philippines is one of the heaviest TikTok markets in the world and Makati PMU competitors are weak there. This is the cheapest reach available to us.

**First, ask Kate**: does she have any personal TikTok account already? If yes and it has real followers, consider renaming it to the brand handle instead of starting from zero. If it's empty or personal, leave it and create fresh.

### B1. Create the account (30 min, helper with Kate present)

1. Sign up at tiktok.com (or the app) using **kate.kanner.pmu@gmail.com** so login codes land where the helper can see them
2. Username, in order of preference (check availability):
   - `@katekannerpmu` (matches the branded hashtag #katekannerpmu)
   - `@kate.kanner.pmu`
   - `@pmubykatekanner`
3. Display name: `Kate Kanner | PMU Makati`
4. Switch to a **Business account**: Profile > Menu > Settings > Account > Switch to Business Account > category **Beauty**
5. Profile photo: the exact same portrait as Instagram (cross-platform recognition)
6. Bio (TikTok limit is 80 characters, this fits):

```
PMU Artist Makati 🇵🇭 Lips•Brows•Eyes | Europe Certified | Book ⤵️
```

7. Website field: `https://www.iconique.com.ph/r/lp/permanent-makeup`. If the link field is not yet available on the new account, write "Booking link in my Instagram bio" as the last bio line and add it as soon as TikTok unlocks it
8. Link the Instagram account to the TikTok profile (Edit profile > Instagram)
9. Turn on 2FA, add the account to the credentials sheet
10. In settings, allow Duets and Stitches (free reach when others react to transformations)

### B2. Pre-launch warm-up (Day 1-2, helper)

Do not post on a brand new cold account immediately:

- [ ] Follow 30-50 relevant accounts: PH beauty creators, Makati businesses, PMU artists worldwide, bridal PH pages
- [ ] Spend 20-30 min/day for 2 days scrolling beauty content, liking and commenting as Kate's account (teaches the algorithm who we are, warms the account)
- [ ] Save 10-15 trending sounds that fit before/after transitions

### B3. Launch sequence (Day 3-7)

New TikTok accounts typically get an exploration reach boost on early posts. Spend it on the strongest material, not an intro video:

- **Post 1**: best before/after transformation we have (re-edited clean, trending audio)
- **Post 2**: client reaction reveal (raw emotion outperforms polish on TikTok)
- **Post 3**: procedure time-lapse with text overlay "Permanent makeup artist in Makati 🇵🇭"
- Then: daily posting per the file 16 calendar
- Announce the TikTok on IG Stories + add the TikTok link to Linktree

### B4. TikTok rules that differ from Instagram

- **Captions are search queries.** TikTok is a search engine for Gen Z and younger millennials. First line of every caption carries the keyword: "permanent makeup Makati", "microblading Philippines", "lip blush healing". Also say the keyword out loud in talking videos and put it in on-screen text, TikTok indexes all three.
- **3-6 hashtags only** (vs 15-25 on IG). Mix: 2 service + 2 local + 1 broad. Example: `#microbladingph #pmuph #makati #beautytokph #permanentmakeup`
- **No cross-platform watermarks.** Both platforms suppress videos carrying the other's logo. Helper edits once in CapCut, exports a clean MP4, uploads natively to TikTok and IG separately (file 16).
- **Trending audio matters more.** Check TikTok Creative Center weekly for trending PH sounds.
- **Reply to comments with video** when a comment asks a real question ("does it hurt?"). Comment-reply videos are the easiest high-performing content on TikTok.
- Posting time PH: 12-1PM or 6-10PM (file 16 sets the exact slot per day).
