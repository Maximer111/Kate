- ✅ Service keywords (microblading, powder eyebrows, ombre brows, combo brows, lip blushing, classic lash line, powder eyeliner)
- ✅ Location keywords (Salcedo Village, Makati, Reverie)
- ✅ Authority signal (10+ years, Europe certified)

- ✅ Male client inclusivity (rare in PH PMU market = competitive advantage)

## Policy reminders

These are ALLOWED in GBP descriptions:
- ✅ City, neighborhood, address
- ✅ Services and specialties
- ✅ Brand mission, history, credentials
- ✅ What makes you unique
- ✅ First-person voice
- ✅ Mention of current studio (Reverie)

These are NOT ALLOWED:
- ❌ Phone numbers
- ❌ URLs / website links
- ❌ Promotional content ("Best in Makati", "Book today!")
- ❌ Pricing or special offers
- ❌ Misleading or exaggerated claims

## How to update

1. Login at business.google.com as Kate
2. Select "Kate Kanner Permanent Makeup"
3. Click "Edit profile" → "About" → "Business description"
4. Paste Version 1
5. Click Save
6. Google takes 1-3 days to approve

## Test before going live

Paste your draft into:
- Character counter (must be under 750)
- Read aloud to check it flows naturally in Kate's voice
- Have Kate herself read it to confirm it sounds like her

## When Kate changes clinics

Update the description by replacing "at Reverie Aesthetic and Hair Center in Salcedo Village" with the new clinic name and location. The rest of the description stays.
