## Things NOT to do

These would slow progress or backfire:

- ❌ Don't create a second GBP at the same address under Kate's name (Google flags as duplicate)
- ❌ Don't offer discounts in exchange for reviews (against Google policy, can suspend GBP)
- ❌ Don't review-swap with other businesses
- ❌ Don't submit to "500 directories" services (low quality, ignored by Google)
- ❌ Don't pay for SEO services from random Upwork freelancers without checking portfolio
- ❌ Don't change branding mid-campaign (consistency matters for ranking)
- ❌ Don't post the same content to IG, TikTok, and Pinterest at the exact same time without adapting (each platform has different best practices)

## Important market data (PH PMU industry)

For pitch decks and strategic context:

- PMU bridal season spikes: Nov-Feb, May-Jun (searches +30-50%)
- 75-80% of PMU clients are women aged 25-45
- Makati + BGC have highest disposable income concentration in PH
- "Near me" searches dominate: ~60% of beauty searches in Metro Manila
- Map pack gets ~70% of clicks on local PH beauty searches
- Top 3 map pack splits ~70% of clicks among them
- Filipino consumers check Facebook BEFORE Google for local businesses (so FB Business page matters)
- TikTok beauty content in PH performs exceptionally well
