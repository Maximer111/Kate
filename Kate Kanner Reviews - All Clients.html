How to use these templates

  • Send 2–3 examples per client, never all 20
  • Tell them: "Here are a few examples, write yours in your own words"
  • Spread reviews over 60–90 days, never all in one burst
  • Mix services across the reviews you collect
  • Coach for personal details (job, brow shape, wedding date)
  • Accept short reviews, they look more real
  • Ask for a photo attached when possible
Don't do this:
  • Have clients copy-paste verbatim (Google detects duplicates)
  • Review-swap with other businesses
  • Ask non-clients to review
  • Offer discounts in exchange for reviews (against Google policy)
