## Client consent rule (non-negotiable)

Before filming any client: Kate asks on camera or has them tick the consent line on the intake form. No consent = no filming, no exceptions. Faces can be cropped/blurred for shy clients; brows/lips close-ups usually don't need the full face. Never post names without permission.

## Kate's standard shot list (every consenting client)

Shot this exact set each time. 5 minutes total on Kate's phone, vertical, 1080p or higher:

1. **Before**: close-up of brows/lips/eyes, good light, 10 sec, slow steady pan
2. **Mapping/design**: 20 sec of Kate drawing the shape
3. **Procedure overhead**: 30-60 sec of the actual work (phone on tripod above)
4. **Wide BTS**: 10 sec of Kate working, shows the studio
5. **The reveal**: client sees result in mirror for the first time. Film their face. This is the highest-value clip we have
6. **After**: close-up matching shot #1 angle
7. When clients return for touch-up: **healed result** close-up (gold for credibility)

One client filmed = material for 3-5 posts. Two clients per week filmed = the whole calendar is covered.

## Weekly batch filming block (60-90 min, once a week)

Kate films 4-5 talking videos in one sitting, phone on tripod, studio background, change top/angle between takes so they don't look identical. The helper sends her the week's scripts the day before (hooks below, full topics in the calendar). Each video: 20-45 seconds, Kate says the keyword out loud ("permanent makeup in Makati") at least once.

## Editing specs (every video, both platforms)

- Vertical 9:16, 1080x1920, exported clean MP4 (no TikTok/IG watermark, edit in CapCut and upload natively to each platform)
- **Hook in the first 1.5 seconds**: text overlay + the most striking frame. Never open with a logo
- Auto-captions ON, most people watch muted. Check spelling of PMU terms
- Length: 15-45 sec sweet spot. Time-lapses 30-60 sec
- Music: pick trending audio separately per platform (IG Reels trending list vs TikTok Creative Center PH)
- Before/after: always same angle, same lighting if possible, hard cut or swipe transition on the beat
- Cover frame: choose deliberately, grid must stay clean (text-on-cover for carousels)
- Color: warm, bright, skin tones natural. No heavy filters on results (misrepresents healed color)

## Caption rules

- **Instagram**: use the exact captions written in file 07, then location line + CTA + full hashtag block from file 07
- **TikTok**: shorter. Formula: `[keyword first line] + [one curiosity or emotion line] + [CTA] + 3-6 hashtags`
  - Keyword first line examples: "Microblading in Makati", "Lip blush healing day 1 vs day 30", "Permanent eyeliner before and after Philippines"
  - TikTok hashtag base set, rotate: `#pmuph #microbladingph #lipblushph #makati #beautytokph #permanentmakeup #fyp`
- CTA always: "Book via link in bio or DM BOOK 📍 Salcedo Village, Makati"
