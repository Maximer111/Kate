# Landing Page Strategy

## The page we work with

**URL:** https://www.iconique.com.ph/r/lp/permanent-makeup

**Branding:** Iconique (not Kate-branded)

**Status:** Working booking page owned by the clinic. Do NOT modify.

## How to use it without owning it

The landing page is the **end of Kate's funnel** — pure conversion. It captures the lead and sends it to the clinic's booking system. Kate's brand happens BEFORE the visitor reaches this page (on IG, TikTok, Pinterest).

### The visitor flow

```
Sees Kate's Reel
     ↓
Follows or DMs Kate
     ↓
Kate replies with personality + booking link
     ↓
Visitor clicks link, lands on Iconique page
     ↓
Fills booking form with "with Kate" in message field
     ↓
Clinic routes booking to Kate
```

## Critical ask of the clinic (5-min change)

Request the clinic add ONE field to the booking form:

**"Preferred artist (optional)"** dropdown with options:
- No preference
- Kate Kanner
- [Other artists]

This routes Kate's bookings directly to her without manual handling. Most clinics will say yes because it benefits them too.

**If they refuse:** Tell clients to write Kate's name in the "Message" or "Notes" field.

## What clients should know before clicking the link

In every DM and post caption, set expectation:

> "Once you book through the link, mention my name (Kate) in the notes so they assign me to you."

This way the Iconique-branded page isn't confusing — clients know they're booking with Kate via Iconique's system.

## Why we're NOT building a separate page for Kate

The user explicitly chose not to. Reasons this works:

✅ The landing page already exists and works
✅ No web development cost
✅ The clinic owns the booking infrastructure
✅ Kate's personal brand lives on social, not on a web property
✅ When she moves, she just changes the link in her bio

## What to monitor

Track in clinic's booking system or by asking the clinic:

- How many bookings come in with "Kate" or "with Kate" in the notes?
- How many bookings come from the landing page traffic vs walk-ins or other artists?
- What % of Kate's monthly bookings originate from Instagram?
