## What NOT to put in GBP description (policy)

- ❌ Phone numbers
- ❌ URLs / website links
- ❌ Promotional phrases ("Best in Makati", "Book now!")
- ❌ Special offers, prices, sales
- ❌ Misleading info

## What IS allowed

- ✅ Mentioning address / neighborhood / city
- ✅ Services offered
- ✅ Personal story, certifications, experience
- ✅ Mention of Reverie as current studio location
