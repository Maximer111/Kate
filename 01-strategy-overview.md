# Strategy Overview

## The hybrid Kate + Reverie approach

Kate Kanner is an independent permanent makeup artist who currently works at Reverie Aesthetic and Hair Center in Salcedo Village, Makati. The Google Business Profile is **"Kate Kanner Permanent Makeup"** — her own personal listing.

This hybrid setup is the best long-term strategy because:

1. **Kate owns the GBP forever.** Reviews, photos, and ranking equity stay with her.
2. **When she changes clinics, the GBP follows her.** She updates the address, keeps everything else.
3. **Reverie's location reputation gives her a launch boost.** Their Plaza Royale address is established and findable.
4. **If she opens her own studio, no rebuild needed.** Same GBP, new address.

## Why this hybrid is strong

- **Reverie reputation borrow:** Reverie's name and Salcedo Village location have established trust. Reviews mentioning both Kate AND Reverie build credibility on both sides.
- **Kate brand build:** Kate's GBP, IG, TikTok, and Pinterest all grow her personal equity.
- **Future-proof:** When Kate moves or opens her own studio, the brand identity stays intact.

## What changes when Kate leaves Reverie

She updates:
- Address on her GBP
- Bio link if Reverie was sending traffic
- Mentions of "Reverie" in upcoming content (gradual phase-out)

She keeps:
- The Kate Kanner Permanent Makeup GBP
- All 343+ followers on IG
- All Google reviews (some mention Reverie, but they're under Kate's name)
- Facebook page
- Booking flow infrastructure she's built
