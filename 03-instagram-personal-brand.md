## Engagement tactics

- **Respond to every DM within 2 hours during business hours**
- **Reply to every comment in the first 60 min after posting** (boosts reach)
- **Use Story polls + question boxes daily** to keep engagement up
- **Save and repost client tags** to your Story within 24 hours
- **Engage on 10-15 local Makati/PH beauty accounts** before posting your own content

## Growth tactics for first 90 days

### Month 1: Foundation
- Post 7x/week consistently
- Use 3-5 trending sounds per week on Reels
- Comment thoughtfully on 20 local beauty accounts daily
- Collab post with 1-2 PH beauty micro-influencers

### Month 2: Authority
- Pitch beauty bloggers (WhenInManila, Spot.ph) for features
- Start a weekly "Healed Results Friday" series
- Run a giveaway (free touch-up to encourage tags + follows)
- Cross-post Reels to TikTok and Pinterest

### Month 3: Scale
- Run small Meta ads (₱200-500/day) targeting Makati women 25-45 with beauty interests
- Guest on PH beauty podcasts or IG Lives
- Partner with bridal vendors (huge for PMU)
- Launch "Refer a friend" discount program

## Metrics to track weekly

- New followers
- Reel reach (target: 5,000+ per Reel by month 2)
- Story views
- DMs received
- Booking inquiries from DMs
- Profile-link clicks to landing page
