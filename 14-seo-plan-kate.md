## Immediate Fixes (Do Today)

### Fix 1 — Remove "Lip fillers" from GBP services
Lip fillers are an injectable medical procedure. Having it listed can mislead clients and trigger Google's medical licensing flags.
> GBP → Edit services → remove "Lip fillers"

### Fix 2 — Remove duplicate services
"Lash tattooing" and "Eyebrow tinting" each appear twice. Remove duplicates.
> GBP → Edit services → remove duplicates

### Fix 3 — Confirm holiday hours (Philippines Independence Day)
Google is showing this prompt. Unconfirmed hours = clients showing up to a closed studio = bad reviews.
> Tap the "Confirm your holiday hours" card on GBP dashboard

### Fix 4 — Complete profile attributes
Go to **Edit profile → More → Attributes** and add:
- Women-owned business
- Appointment required
- Payment methods accepted
- Wheelchair accessible (yes/no)
